# ADR 0002: Handbag-First MVP

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Catalog video generation can expand into many product and apparel cases. A broad first scope would dilute product preservation work.

## Decision

The MVP supports handbags only and targets a curated demo set of 8-12 entries.

## Consequences

The pipeline can focus on silhouette, strap, hardware, and logo preservation. Apparel fit, CatVTON, and try-on workflows are future work.
