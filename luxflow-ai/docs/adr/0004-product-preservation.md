# ADR 0004: Product Preservation

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The handbag is the commercial subject. Diffusion can easily distort logos, handles, straps, hardware, and proportions.

## Decision

Product preservation is modeled as a first-class layer with freeze policy, future masks, compositing, and similarity checks.

## Consequences

Generation quality is judged by product faithfulness, not only cinematic appeal. More implementation work is required before real media output is acceptable.
