# MVP Scope

The MVP is handbag-first because handbags are high-value catalog subjects with clear silhouettes and preservation requirements. This keeps the first demo focused on 8-12 curated catalog entries instead of a combinatorial content matrix.

Included:

- Handbag product metadata.
- Synthetic or licensed editorial model profiles.
- Location and action metadata.
- Declarative scene recipe compilation.
- Engine-agnostic route selection.
- Product preservation policies.
- React workflow inspector.
- Placeholder catalog entries and evaluation notes.

Cut from MVP:

- Apparel route and CatVTON.
- Gradio or Streamlit.
- Real-time product detector.
- Full QA suite.
- Real image or video generation.
- User auth.
- SaaS features.
- Celery, Redis, Docker, Prometheus, or Grafana.
