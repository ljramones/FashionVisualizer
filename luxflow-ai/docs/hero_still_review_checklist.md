# Hero-Still Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing prompt tuning contact sheets.

- Adult model appears natural and editorial.
- Location matches the selected scene.
- Action or motion is plausible.
- No actual handbag, purse, luggage, or fake branded accessory is generated.
- Right hand and right hip area are visible and usable for later product placement.
- Hands are not severely distorted.
- No obvious logos, watermarks, text, or fake brand marks.
- Portrait framing leaves enough room for the product layer.
- Lighting is compatible with later compositing.
